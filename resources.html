Resources

A compilation of sustainability-related resources:

McGill

Sustainability Projects Fund

The Sustainability Projects Fund was established to facilitate the development and offer financial backing for initiatives that promote sustainability on the McGill campus. This initiative aims to actively involve the McGill community in various sustainability projects. Find out more here.

Academics

Environmental Engineering

McGill provides various platforms for the study of environmental science and sustainability. The Department of Civil Engineering offers a specialization in Environmental Engineering, catering to both undergraduate and graduate students. Moreover, students within the Faculty of Engineering and the Department of Bioresource Engineering have the opportunity to pursue a minor in Environmental Engineering.

Montréal

Sustainable Montréal 2016-2020

"Sustainable Montréal 2016-2020" is the third and current sustainable development plan aimed at transforming Montréal into a sustainable metropolis. To delve deeper into the background of this approach, partner organizations, progress monitoring, and local sustainable development plans, you can find more information here.
